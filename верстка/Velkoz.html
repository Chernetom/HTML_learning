Vel'Koz

t is unclear if Vel'Koz Vel'Koz was the first Voidborn to emerge on Runeterra, but there has certainly never been another to match his level of cruel, calculating sentience. While his kin devour or defile everything around them, he seeks instead to scrutinize and study the physical realm—and the strange, warlike beings that dwell there—for any weakness the Void Crest Void might exploit. But Vel'Koz is far from a passive observer, striking back at threats with deadly plasma, or by disrupting the very fabric of the world itself.
Vel'Koz is a slender, pale-skinned woman with long dark brown hair. Her robes are composed of a Void Symbiote with a distinct palette of grey, dark grey, purple and bronze. The most distinctive ornaments of her attire are some purple marks on her face.

Spells

ACTIVE: Vel'Koz fires a plasma bolt in the target direction that deals magic damage to the first enemy hit, Slow slowing them by 70% decaying over a few seconds. Plasma Fission can be recast after 0.25 seconds while the bolt is in flight, and does so automatically upon hitting an enemy or maximum range.
ACTIVE: Vel'Koz cracks a rift in the target direction that opens after a 0.25-second delay, cascading through the area over 0.65 seconds to deal magic damage to enemies within.
ACTIVE: Vel'Koz hurls a disruptive anomaly that lands at the target location after 0.75 seconds, which deals magic damage to enemies hit and Airborne knocks them up and Stun stuns them for 0.75 seconds, as well as granting Sight sight of the area briefly.
ACTIVE: After a 0.2-second delay, Vel'Koz Channeling channels for up to 2.6 seconds to project an energy beam, during which he can steer the beam in the target direction. Life Form Disintegration Ray can be recast after 1 second during the channel, and does so automatically after the duration.
